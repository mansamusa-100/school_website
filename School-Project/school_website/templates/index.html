Empowering Students for a Bright Future

About Us

Mansa Bilingual Academy is a comprehensive educational institution dedicated to nurturing and developing the potential of every student from early childhood through to young adulthood. With a robust and dynamic curriculum, we offer a seamless educational journey from Nursery through to Senior High School.

Nursery School

Our Nursery School provides a warm and nurturing environment for our youngest learners, aged 2 to 5. We focus on early childhood development through play-based learning, fostering social, emotional, cognitive, and physical growth. Our experienced and caring teachers ensure each child feels safe, valued, and excited about learning.

Primary School

In our Primary School, for children aged 6 to 12, we lay a strong academic foundation while encouraging creativity and critical thinking. Our curriculum includes core subjects such as Mathematics, English, Science, and Social Studies, complemented by arts, physical education, and technology. We emphasize developing literacy and numeracy skills, fostering a love for learning, and building confidence.

Junior High School

Our Junior High School, catering to students aged 13 to 15, bridges the transition from primary education to more specialized senior studies. The curriculum expands to include advanced subjects and elective courses, allowing students to explore their interests and talents. We focus on developing problem-solving abilities, critical thinking, and effective communication skills, preparing students for the challenges of Senior High School.

Senior High School

At the Senior High School level, for students aged 16 to 18, we offer a rigorous academic program designed to prepare students for higher education and future careers. Our curriculum includes a wide range of subjects, with options for specialization in sciences, humanities, or vocational studies. We provide guidance and support for college applications, career planning, and personal development. Our goal is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in their chosen paths

Facilities and Extracurricular Activities

Our school boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, science and computer labs, libraries, sports fields, and arts studios. We offer a diverse range of extracurricular activities, from sports and music to debate clubs and community service programs, ensuring a well-rounded education

Mission and Values

At Mansa Bilingual Academy, we are committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive community where every student can thrive. Our mission is to inspire a love for learning, promote academic excellence, and instill values of integrity, respect, and responsibility. We strive to develop lifelong learners who are prepared to make positive contributions to society.

Join us at Mansa Bilingual Academy and be a part of our vibrant educational community, where we nurture minds and shape futures
